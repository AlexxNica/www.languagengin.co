languagengine

Adding voice control to your app can be as quick as...

// Objective-C

#import "LanguageEngine.h"

LanguageEngine *le = [LanguageEngine
                      controller: myApp
                      lexicon: @{
                          Nouns: @[@"tweet", @"reply"],
                          Verbs: @[@"respond", @"tell"]
                      }];

// Java

import LanguageEngine;

LanguageEngine le = new LanguageEngine(myApp,
                                       {"tweet", "reply"},
                                       {"respond", "tell"});

Easy

Add voice control to your app with minimal effort.

With languagengine, adding voice control to your app takes just a few lines of code. All of the hard work is handled inside LE, so you don't have to worry.

Flexible

Extend languagengine with new words and meanings specific to your app.

languagengine is designed to be generic, so you can customize its vocabulary by adding the nouns and verbs that fit the user experience you want to create.

Intuitive

Using natural English makes it easy for anyone to start using voice control.

Because languagengine uses natural English rather than an artificial subset of English, using LE voice controls is a nearly transparent experience.

Conversational

languagengine lets users talk with your app, not merely at it.

Verbal communication isn't just a pile of words, it's a conversation. languagengine allows users to converse with your app, permitting richer interactions.
